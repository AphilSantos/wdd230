Our Story


Welcome to Bountiful Foods! We are a small business based in Carlsbad, California, specializing in organic, locally grown foods. Our food trucks serve the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego, offering fresh produce from local farmers and select national/international suppliers. Whether you visit our Carlsbad storefront, call us, or order online, we're dedicated to providing convenient access to healthy options. Join us in embracing a wholesome lifestyle with our made-to-order fruit drinks and bags. Experience the abundance of organic bounty and the convenience of Bountiful Foods.
