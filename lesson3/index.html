Kagay-anon Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The main objective of this website is to serve as a platform for entrepreneurs and business owners to connect with each other and foster growth within their local community. Our website offers a plethora of resources including a directory of members, the benefits of joining our organization, upcoming events, and demographic and historical information about the City of Cagayan de Oro.

Despite being a humble City, Cagayan de Oro boasts many attractions such as the Macahambus Cave and a rich cultural history that is sure to captivate visitors. Our website also aims to attract new businesses to the city, thereby contributing to the municipal's development and providing new retail and dining options for the community.

By creating a vibrant business community, we aim to foster local economic growth and create a thriving ecosystem for both established and emerging businesses. We believe that by working together, we can achieve greater success and prosperity for the city of Cagayan de Oro.
