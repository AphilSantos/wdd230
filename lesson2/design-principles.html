White Space and Clean Design

Apple Website

Apple.com

The Apple website has a good use of white space, which is an important aspect of a clean and minimalist design. The white space on the Apple website serves to separate different elements on the page, making it easier for the user to focus on the most important information.
For example, on the homepage of the Apple website, the top section of the page has a lot of white space around the main heading and image. This helps to draw attention to these elements and make them stand out on the page.

Proximity

Airbnb Website

Airbnb.com

Airbnb is an excellent example of a website that follows good proximity principles. Proximity refers to the practice of placing related elements close together to create visual harmony and establish a clear visual hierarchy.
Airbnb uses proximity to create a clean and organized layout that reduces cognitive load for the user. For instance, the website's navigation menu is well-spaced and grouped together, making it easy for users to scan and find what they are looking for.
